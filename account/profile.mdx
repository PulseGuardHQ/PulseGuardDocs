---
title: 'Managing Your Profile'
description: 'How to update your personal information and change your password.'
---

You can manage your personal details from your profile settings.

## Updating Profile Information

1.  Click on your avatar in the top right corner and select **Profile**.
2.  Here you can update your name and email address.
3.  Click **Save** to apply the changes.

## Changing Your Password

For security reasons, it's a good practice to regularly update your password.

1.  Go to the **Security** tab within your profile settings.
2.  Enter your current password and your new password.
3.  Confirm the new password and click **Update Password**.

<Note>
If you've forgotten your password, you can use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
</Note>
