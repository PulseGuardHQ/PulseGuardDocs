## Overview

PulseGuard's AI Insights feature provides intelligent analysis of your monitoring data to identify patterns, predict issues, and provide recommendations. Our AI engine helps you stay ahead of potential problems and optimize your web resources through advanced analytics.

<Note>
  AI insights are available on Pro plans (limited) and Expert plans (full access). The AI features require at least 7 days of monitoring data to provide meaningful insights.
</Note>
