## Welcome to PulseGuard

PulseGuard is a comprehensive monitoring solution designed to help you keep track of your websites, APIs, and devices. This documentation will guide you through all the features and functionalities of the application.

<Note>
  This documentation assumes you are using the latest version of PulseGuard. Some features may not be available in older versions.
</Note>

## Key Features

PulseGuard offers a range of tools and features to ensure your digital assets are always performing optimally:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Real-time Monitoring" icon="satellite-dish">
    Monitor websites and APIs with checks every 30 seconds to 5 minutes depending on your plan
  </Card>
  <Card title="SSL Certificate Monitoring" icon="shield-check">
    Track SSL certificate validity and receive alerts before certificates expire
  </Card>
  <Card title="Server Monitoring" icon="server">
    Monitor server and device performance including CPU, memory, and disk space
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI-powered Insights" icon="brain">
    Get intelligent analysis and anomaly detection with our AI engine
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting Started

Get started with PulseGuard in just a few simple steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up for a PulseGuard account or log in to your existing account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a domain or device">
    Add your first domain or device for monitoring
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure notifications">
    Set up your notification preferences to stay informed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore the dashboard">
    Navigate through the dashboard to view your monitoring data
  </Step>
</Steps>
